Frequently Asked Questions

Processing time varies by country. Tourist visas typically take 3-15 working days. We recommend applying at least 3-4 weeks before your travel date.
Common requirements include valid passport, photographs, bank statements, ITR, cover letter, and travel itinerary. Specific requirements vary by country.
While we cannot guarantee approval (final decision rests with the embassy), our 98% success rate reflects our expertise in preparing strong applications.
