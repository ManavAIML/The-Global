Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about visa services

How long does the visa process take?
Processing time varies by country. Some visas like Thailand (on arrival) are instant, while USA/UK visas may take 2-5 weeks. We recommend applying at least 3-4 weeks before your travel date.
What documents are generally required?
Common documents include: Valid passport with 6+ months validity, recent photographs, bank statements (3-6 months), employment/business proof, travel itinerary, and hotel bookings. Specific requirements vary by country.
Do you guarantee visa approval?
While we cannot guarantee approval (as it's the embassy's decision), we ensure proper documentation and application to maximize your chances. We have a 95%+ approval rate across all destinations.
What if my visa is rejected?
In case of rejection, we help analyze the reason and guide you on reapplication. Service fees are non-refundable, but embassy fees may be refundable depending on the country's policy.
Can you help with urgent/emergency visas?
Yes, we offer expedited processing for urgent travel. Additional charges may apply. Contact us immediately with your requirements and we'll do our best to assist.
